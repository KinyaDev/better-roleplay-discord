Better Roleplay Bot - Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how the Better Roleplay bot ("the Bot") handles and protects user data collected during its operation. By adding the Bot to your Discord server, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy.

  1. Data Collection:

    • The Bot may collect and store certain user data to provide its functionalities and improve the user experience.
    • The collected data may include user IDs, character information, and server-specific settings related to the Bot's features.

  2. Data Usage:

    • The collected user data is used solely for the purpose of enabling and enhancing the features provided by the Bot within the Discord server.
    • The Bot developer does not sell or share user data with third parties.

  3. Data Security:

    • The Bot developer takes reasonable measures to ensure the security of user data collected by the Bot.
    • The Bot developer implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of user data.

  4. Data Retention:

    • The Bot retains user data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy or as required by law.

  5. User Rights:

    • Users have the right to request access to their personal data collected by the Bot and to request its deletion.
    • Users can exercise these rights by contacting the Bot developer.

  6. Children's Privacy:

    • The Bot is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 ("Children").
    • The Bot developer does not knowingly collect personal information from Children. If you are a parent or guardian and you become aware that your Child has provided personal data to the Bot, please contact the Bot developer to have the data removed.

  7. Changes to the Privacy Policy:

    • This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Users will be notified of any material changes by the Bot developer.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact the Bot developer.
